Parth Limbani

I am a mechanical design engineer based in Kutchh, Gujarat, with over 6 years of hands-on experience in CAD drafting, 3D modeling, industrial automation, and engineering simulation. My goal is to bridge the gap between design intent and manufacturing reality for local industries.

Galaxy Automation was founded with a clear mission: to bring big-company engineering capability to small and mid-sized manufacturers in Kutchh — at accessible cost, with local support, and without compromising on precision or quality.

I have worked with fabrication units, packaging machinery manufacturers, pump companies, export-oriented units, and conveyor system builders — understanding their unique challenges and delivering solutions that work on the shop floor.
